DAILY TROJAN

GRAPHIC DESIGNER
2021 - PRESENT

A growing gallery of spot illustrations, digital mockups, and sport graphics that were completed for University of Southern California’s student-run school newspaper, the Daily Trojan, throughout my employment as a designer during the 2021-2022 school year.

SPECIAL EDITION COVER DESIGNS

I’ve been given the amazing opportunity to design the covers for multiple issues. These designs typically drew upon topical news, school events, and included articles within the particular issue of the Daily Trojan. The process normally includes multiple rounds of feedback from the senior editors at Daily Trojan. It’s been incredibly challenging to accurately address the theme of an issue, but incredibly awarding to see my work printed on such a large scale.

SPOT ILLUSTRATIONS

I’ve also created smaller spot illustrations to be included alongside featured opinion articles. These illustrations focused on accurately foreshadowing the material that’s covered within the specific piece, allowing readers to quickly grasp the topic of each article before choosing to read more about the subject.
