WHOLE CHIPS

PRODUCT DESIGN
10 WEEKS, FALL 2021

Whole Chips is an Indonesia based snack company that is committed to bringing ethically sourced, nutritious alternatives to people’s snacking diets. They reached out to USC’s creative agency club, Innovative Design (InnoD), for help with redesigning their current e-commerce website as they prepare to expand into the North American market. I worked alongside 3 other members of InnoD’s Web team (Michelle Wang, Colin Ho, Louis Chen) to design and develop their new website.

CONTENT AUDIT & INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE

We started off with a Content Audit by dissecting the existing website. We realized that there were too many unnecessary features and screens, and realized we wanted to provide a cleaner and more streamlined experience. We implemented these changes when reorganizing the site’s Information Architecture.

MOODBOARD

We then created Moodboards to understand the aesthetics of the website. The client wanted their rebranding to be kid-friendly, with bright, saturated colors. We agreed on a combination of the existing color palette and the newer, proposed palette.

PROTOTYPES

After several low-fi prototype iterations, my finalized high fidelity prototype was submitted to the client along with other prototypes created by my other team members. The Whole Chips team A/B tested our final designs, and found that the target customer preferred a warmer purple color scheme.
