AV TECHNOLOGY

CASE STUDY
***

An understatement: I’ve been excited about the advances in autonomous vehicle (AV) technology for years. Just a decade ago, AV technology made their first appearance on the news. Now, in 2022, all of the largest tech companies have invested a portion of their resources into this competitive arms race. Of course, the largest tech companies aren’t entirely alone. A new crop of startups have also joined this saturated market, bringing forth autonomous taxis, driverless 16 wheeler trucks, drone-powered delivery systems, and more. It’s impossible to talk about some of the new changes in the tech space without talking about the exciting future of AV technology.

To be frank, this dump of scraps and notes doubles as a love letter to a growing space that I aspire to design in. It’s a confession caked with potential user journeys, messy scribbles, and half baked ideas. Everything is in here—even the crazy ones.

That said, please note that I am just an overly excited college student and UIUX designer. I am no expert in AV technology. But hopefully, one day, I’ll get some first-hand experience in this field.
