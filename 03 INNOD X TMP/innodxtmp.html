INNOD X TMP

WEB TEAM LEAD
10 WEEKS, FALL 2022

Trojan Marketplace (TMP) is a biannual, student-run market event featuring student-owned small businesses. They aim to uplift the entrepreneurship community at USC by featuring 50+ businesses each semester.

TMP reached out to USC’s creative agency club, Innovative Design (InnoD), for help with designing and creating their website from scratch. I lead a small team (Arella Yang, Cynthia Chen, Dayon Lee, Teresa Tran) to design and develop this new website from head to toe in a span of 10 weeks.

I was responsible for leading my team through the process of creating a website and developing a brand identity while also teaching Figma basics, introducing Webflow’s interface, and communicating with the clients. I spent off hours working with team members 1 on 1 to build their confidence as designers, to provide advice about portfolios and reviewing case studies, and to answer any questions big or small (what’s the difference between agency and in-house? what’s the difference between good UI and great UI?).
