USC LAVALAB

LavaLab is a student-run product incubator at the University of Southern California that provides students with the opportunity to create their own startups. Each team is composed of 4 members: 1 product manager (Aashish Suresh), 2 developers (Andrea Lee, SJ Na), and 1 designer (me!), and have approximately 10 weeks to create a MVP to pitch to VCs and investors.

My team has created Pretzel, a one-stop platform that brings together small restaurants and social media influencers to create exciting content.
